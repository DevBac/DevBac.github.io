Who We Are

Our Story

As a class project Bachelor was developed by Esrat Ema, Asrafi Akter, Salekur Rahaman and Ania Chowdury in 2020 in Dhaka city. Some of us were bachelors and we had so many problems in this bachelor's life. For these reasons, we tried to make bachelor's life easier to help them in their daily life by calculating and manage meals, handling personal and educational tasks and creating a better communicating platform.
